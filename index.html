Welcome

Our family heritage is Kashmiri. Authentic North Indian recipes are therefore what we know, and love they are our foundation. Handed down by generation upon generation, we take this intricate food heritage and put our own twist on the recipes, creating something at once traditional and new. Our Head Chef Minal conjures up new and creative twists on the foundational recipes we've inherited.
